Understanding Live Exchange Rates

Exchange rates represent the value of one currency compared to another. These rates fluctuate constantly based on global forex markets, economic indicators, interest rates, inflation, trade balance, and geopolitical events. Our live currency converter fetches updated rates in real time to ensure accurate conversion.

What Factors Affect Currency Exchange Rates?

  • Central bank interest rate decisions
  • Inflation and economic growth
  • Political stability and global trade policies
  • Supply and demand in the forex market
  • International investment flows

Why Bank Rates May Differ from Market Rates

Banks and forex providers often add a margin or service charge to the interbank rate. This is why the rate shown on Google or a currency converter may slightly differ from what banks offer. For large transactions, always check with your financial institution.
